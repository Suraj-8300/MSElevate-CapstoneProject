## 📌 Project Overview
[cite_start]This project, developed as part of the **Microsoft Elevate AICTE Internship Capstone Project**, focuses on analyzing retail sales data from Big Mart[cite: 1, 5]. [cite_start]The primary goal is to transform large volumes of raw data into meaningful business insights to identify high-performing products, efficient outlet types, and location-based sales trends[cite: 25, 30, 31].

## 📊 Key Insights & Results
[cite_start]Based on the dashboard analysis, several critical business patterns emerged[cite: 243, 244]:
* [cite_start]**Top Product Category:** Fruits and Vegetables generate the highest sales among all categories[cite: 245].
* [cite_start]**Dominant Outlet Type:** Supermarket Type 1 outlets contribute the largest share of total revenue[cite: 246].
* [cite_start]**Location Performance:** Tier 3 locations show higher sales performance compared to Tier 1 and Tier 2[cite: 247].
* [cite_start]**Store Size Impact:** Medium-sized outlets account for the majority of total sales (63.91%)[cite: 142, 248].
* [cite_start]**Pricing Strategy:** Product pricing (MRP) significantly influences sales, with mid-range priced products performing best[cite: 249].
